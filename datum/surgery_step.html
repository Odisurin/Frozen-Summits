surgery_step
/datum/surgery_step

Check /mob/living/carbon/attackby for how surgery progresses, and also /mob/living/carbon/attack_hand. Other important variables are: var/list/surgeries (/mob/living) var/list/internal_organs (/mob/living/carbon) var/list/bodyparts (/mob/living/carbon)

Vars

accept_any_itemDoes the surgery step accept any item? If true, ignores implements. Compatible with accept_hand.
accept_handDoes the surgery step accept open hand? If true, ignores implements. Compatible with accept_any_item.
chems_neededlist of chems needed to complete the step. Even on success, the step will have no effect if there aren't the chems required in the mob.
descDescription of the surgery step
ignore_clothesThis surgery ignores clothes on the targeted bodypart
implementsTypepaths or tool behaviors that can be used to perform this surgery step, associated to success chance
implements_speedTypepaths or tool behaviors that can be used to perform this surgery step, associated to speed modification
lying_requiredDoes the patient need to be lying down?
nameName of the surgery step
possible_intentsIntents that can be used to perform this surgery step
possible_locsBody zones this surgery can be performed on, set to null for everywhere
repeatingRepeatable surgery steps will repeat until failure
replaced_bytype; doesn't show up if this type exists. Set to /datum/surgery_step if you want to hide a "base" surgery (useful for typing parents IE healing.dm just make sure to null it out again)
require_all_chemsAny chem on the list required, or all of them?
required_organsSome surgeries require specific organs to be present in the patient
requires_bodypartDoes this step require a non-missing bodypart? Incompatible with requires_missing_bodypart
requires_bodypart_typeWhat type of bodypart we require, in case requires_bodypart
requires_missing_bodypartDoes this step require the bodypart to be missing? (Limb attachment)
requires_real_bodypartIf true, this surgery step cannot be done on pseudo limbs (like chainsaw arms)
requires_techHandles techweb-oriented surgeries
self_operableDoes this step allow self surgery?
silicons_obey_probSilicons will ignore the probability of success and always succeed
skill_bonusesModifiers to success chance when you're above the median
skill_malusesModifiers to success chance when you're below the median
skill_medianSkill median used to apply success and speed bonuses
skill_minNecessary skill MINIMUM to perform this surgery step, of skill_used
skill_usedSkill used to perform this surgery step
surgery_flagsRandom surgery flags that mostly indicate additional requirements
surgery_flags_blockedRandom surgery flags blocking certain flags
target_mobtypesAcceptable mob types for this surgery
timeHow long does the step take for someone with average skill and an average tool?
